Why the app is developing?

Main Idea, why the app is developing is, that nowadays almost every family have at least 2 cars and problem with parking is more and more crucial. People are wasting often more than 15 minutes to find parking place near their houses, working places or in the downtowns.

All other apps are providing only paid parking places with high hourly rates. Until now we didn't find any service on the market, where people will inform the realtime information about free parking . Main problem is, that only few drivers are willing to provide these information for free.




What is Spotee?

Spotee is the revolutionary application which will solve your daily problems with parking.

In our app will meet people, which would like to find the parking place for a minimal costs and on the other side are people, they just leaving their spots and they would like to earn extra money on this. Except this main idea this app will allow you to store vehicle’s parked position.

This app will combine three services: Searching for parking spot; Earning money with parking spot and Saving position of your parked vehicle.

Details to Earning service pricing

You can use our earning service first month without any payment,

If you would like to use our earning service also next month, you need to pay the fee for the previous period. 

The fee is calculated from the amount of how much did you earn in total for previous period.

The fee rate starts from 25% until 10% and depends on total earned money with the app.

That means, more you will use this service, less fee rate you will pay,

Another way, how to decrease fee rate is to share this app with your friends, colleagues, relatives.... 


Example of calculating monthly fee:

You used an earning service for the previous month and you earned the cash in total 20 Eur.

If you would like to use the earning service also on the next month, you need to pay only 5Eur fee (20 Eur x 25%).

All other services are totally free and you can use them, if you pay this fee, or not.
